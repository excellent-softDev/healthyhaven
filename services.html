Our Services

We offer a range of services to support your reproductive health, including clinic-based care and home visit options. Explore our services below to learn more.

Insertion & Removal of Contraceptives

We provide professional insertion and removal of contraceptive devices such as IUDs, implants, and diaphragms. Our trained healthcare providers ensure the process is safe, comfortable, and tailored to your needs.

Consultation Bookings

Book consultations with our experts to discuss your contraceptive options, reproductive health concerns, or any questions you may have. Both in-clinic and virtual consultations are available for your convenience.

Treatment of Infections

We diagnose and treat infections such as bacterial vaginosis, yeast infections, and sexually transmitted infections (STIs). Our team ensures confidentiality and provides personalized care.

Home Visits

For your convenience, we offer home visits for contraceptive counseling, device insertion or removal, and follow-up care. Our healthcare providers bring professional services to the comfort of your home.

Educational Workshops

We conduct workshops to educate individuals and groups about contraceptive methods, reproductive health, and safe practices. These workshops are available both in-person and online.

Postpartum Contraceptive Care

We provide postpartum contraceptive counseling and services to help new mothers choose the best contraceptive method for their needs and lifestyle.
